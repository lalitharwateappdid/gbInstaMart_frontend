Journey Table for GBMart

2014

Foundation: GBMart was established in Mumbai to serve the B2B FMCG sector.

2015

Expansion to B2C: Introduced a platform for consumers to directly purchase groceries and FMCG products.

2016

Regional Offices: Opened regional offices in Delhi, Bangalore, and Kolkata to enhance operations.

Product Diversification: Expanded offerings to include cosmetic and herbal products.

Customer Support: Established 24/7 customer support to enhance customer experience.
